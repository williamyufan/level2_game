Race Car

by: William Guo

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I thought a race car game would be very cool, cooler than shooting aliens and emus. I personally love race car games, they are fun. And also, I felt like a racing game would be easy to make for level 2.

2. What I Learned

They gave lots of good feedbacks, like adding power-ups and power-downs to my game.

3. Architecture of My Game

I have a car (duh), I have obstacles that spawns in more and more as the game progress, and I have power-ups and power-downs that can speed up the car or slow the car down. I have three interface, just like League Invaders. Most of my code is in GamePanel, and most classes are extended from my GameObject class.

4. Technical Implementation

I drew my own car and obstacles.I went on to pixel art and spent a class just trying to draw obstacles and cars the right size, it was hard, but it was fun.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging part is the obstacles. The obstacles are strange-shaped, so it was hard to add collision-boxes to the obstacles (I often had to add many collision-boxes to one obstacle). All the collision-box adding took a lot of time, but I overcame it by having my patience (I almost gave up). Now, looking back, I think they are really worth it and they add a pretty nice effect to the obstacles (play the game to see what I mean by cool effect).

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I would make the game look better because right now, it looks very simple and very pixelly. I think I would add some extra effects to make the game even more interesting
